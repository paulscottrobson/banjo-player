File Format (.bnote)
====================

Bars seperated by |

Notes seperated by :

Notes are A-Z representing Fretting 0-25 or . (no pluck). 5 letters or dots, string 1 (bottom string)
first.

Assignments are lines containing

def := constant

Simplest (.banjo1)
==================
<chord> := 01223 is fretting for string 1-string 5
<bars> are | seperated

1-5 means strum 1-5 P plucks 1+5 [<chord>] switches chords. Defaults to G and keeps going unless otherwise
All 8 beat bars
